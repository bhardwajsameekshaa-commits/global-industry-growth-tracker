# 🎨 APAC Paint and Coatings Market Insights

## 📊 Overview
The APAC paint and coatings market is a major contributor to global industry growth, driven by rapid urbanization, infrastructure development, and expanding industrial activities.

- The global paint and coatings market is expected to exceed **USD 250 billion by 2030**  
- Asia-Pacific accounts for over **45% of global demand**

Asia-Pacific continues to dominate the coatings industry due to cost-efficient production and increasing domestic consumption.

- Industrial coatings demand is rising due to manufacturing expansion
